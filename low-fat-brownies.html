Devilishly Good Brownies

The low-fat option

This recipe uses Greek yogurt instead of butter and darker chocolate (with more cocoa solids) to
reduce the fat content. Bake these and you won't feel so guilty about going back for a second one.

PREP TIME
40 min
TOTAL TIME
1 hr 15 min
SERVINGS
A LOT

INGREDIENTS

  • 100g bar dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids, in squares
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 75g each golden caster sugar and light muscovado sugar
  • ½tsp coffee, dissolved in 1 tbsp hot water
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 90g 0% fat Greek yogurt
  • 90g plain flour
  • ¼tsp baking powder
  • 15g cocoa powder
  • Icing sugar, for dusting

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Set the oven to 180°C/350°F/Gas Mark 4.
  2. Melt chocolate over a pan of hot water. Cool for 5 mins.
  3. Whisk the eggs with the sugar until light.
  4. Stir in the chocolate, then the coffee, vanilla and yogurt.
  5. Gently fold in the sifted flour, baking powder and cocoa powder.
  6. Smooth the mixture into the tin.
  7. Bake for 25-30 mins. Leave to cool.
  8. Cut into 16 squares. Dust with icing sugar.
