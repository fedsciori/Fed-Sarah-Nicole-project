Devilishly Good Brownies

The gluten-free option

This recipe uses gluten-free flour, so you can forget about bloated bellies
or discomfort and take a second helping.

PREP TIME
40 min
TOTAL TIME
1 hr 15 min
SERVINGS
A LOT

INGREDIENTS

  • 170 grams (3/4 cup) unsalted butter, melted
  • 200 grams (1 cup) caster sugar
  • 90 grams (1/2 cup) brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 40 grams (1/2 cup) cocoa powder
  • 70 grams (1/2 cup) gluten free (all purpose) plain flour
  • 75 grams milk or dark chocolate chips - Make sure these are also gluten free

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 160 C (320 F). Grease and line an 8 inch square baking tin with baking or parchment paper, ensuring two sides overhang.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, add the melted butter and sugars and gently whisk together. Add the vanilla extract and stir.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, stirring in-between, then sift in the cocoa powder and flour. Stir until just combined. Add chocolate chips.
  4. Pour the brownie batter in the prepared tin and place in the oven.
  5. Bake brownies for approximately 30-35 minutes or until they no longer wobble in the middle.
  6. Leave in tin and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. These brownies are quite fragile so if you can, transfer to the fridge for an hour before cutting into squares to serve.
